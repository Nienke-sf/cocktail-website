Your Favorite Cocktail Bar

Sips Galore is a London-based premium cocktail service creating luxury bar experiences for events that demand impact. From weddings to corporate functions, we don’t just serve drinks — we design visually stunning, high-quality cocktail moments your guests will remember.

Every detail is tailored to your vision, from bespoke menus to fully styled bar setups. Bespoke cocktail & mocktail menus Luxury mobile bar setups Custom branding & themed styling Professional mixologists Available across the UK

Our Mission

At Sips Galore, our mission is simple — to turn every event into an unforgettable experience. With over 100 successful events delivered across London, we bring a fully portable bar, professional bartenders, and live cocktail making directly to you.

We are passionate about crafting unique, high-quality cocktails that not only taste amazing but also look stunning. With elegant displays designed to match your theme, to high-end glassware, florals, and personalised presentation, every detail is handled with care. We manage everything — staff, setup, and drinks — so you can focus on enjoying your moment. When you book Sips Galore, you're not just hiring a bar. You're investing in something extraordinary.
